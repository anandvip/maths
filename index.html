üé≤ Pattern #2: Partition Magic

Imagine you have 5 cookies. How many different ways can you share them?

Every 7th partition is divisible by 7!

Why it matters: These patterns help in coding theory, cryptography, and even understanding how particles arrange themselves in physics!
